Moderate Depression Level

Description

You may be experiencing early symptoms of depression, such as low energy, lack of motivation, and Moderate sadness. Implementing self-help strategies can improve your mood and prevent worsening symptoms.

Signs of Moderate Depression

  • Feeling down or unmotivated.
  • Reduced interest in hobbies and activities.
  • Fatigue or changes in sleep patterns.
  • Difficulty concentrating.

    • Self-Help Strategies

  • Maintain a structured daily routine.
  • Engage in activities that bring joy and relaxation.
  • Connect with loved ones for emotional support.
  • Try journaling to process emotions and thoughts.
  • Seek professional guidance if symptoms persist.
