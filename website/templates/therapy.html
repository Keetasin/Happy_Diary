Severe Depression Level

Description

You may be facing serious depression, which can significantly impact your daily life. Seeking professional therapy and medical support is highly recommended.

Signs of Severe Depression

  • Persistent feelings of sadness or hopelessness.
  • Extreme fatigue and lack of energy.
  • Loss of interest in activities once enjoyed.
  • Significant changes in sleep or appetite.
  • Thoughts of self-harm or suicide (urgent help required).

    • Seeking Help

  • Talk to a trusted therapist or counselor.
  • Consider medical treatments such as therapy or medication.
  • Join support groups to connect with others.
  • Engage in positive coping mechanisms like art, music, or writing.
